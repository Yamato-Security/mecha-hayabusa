## Why Mecha Hayabusa?

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-robot:{ .lg .middle } __AI-powered investigation__

    ---

    Explore [Hayabusa](https://github.com/Yamato-Security/hayabusa) results with an AI assistant in
    natural language, over the **Model Context Protocol** (e.g. Claude).

-   :material-database-cog:{ .lg .middle } __Dataset operations__

    ---

    Load, manage and operate on Hayabusa result datasets so the AI can reason over them.

-   :material-magnify:{ .lg .middle } __Query & search__

    ---

    Query and search across results to zero in on the events that matter.

-   :material-timeline-clock:{ .lg .middle } __Timeline & analytics__

    ---

    Build timelines and run analytics to understand the shape of an incident.

-   :material-shield-bug:{ .lg .middle } __Detail & IOC analysis__

    ---

    Drill into event details, decode artifacts (e.g. Base64 PowerShell), and extract IOCs.

-   :material-file-document-edit:{ .lg .middle } __Report generation__

    ---

    Generate DFIR timelines and reports from your Hayabusa results.

</div>
